About WaXoBe JOAD.

WaXoBe is an archery range located in South Brunswick, New Jersey. Our indoor range has a total of 20 lanes and the distance to the target is 20 yards. JOAD is a training session for kids from 8 to 18 years, run by qualified coaches.

Prior to the World pandemic, we ran two 1 hour beginner sessions on Saturday. The first at 3:30pm and the second at 4:30pm. Students would sign up for a 10 week training session and we would run 3 such sessions in a year.

Given that this pandemic is not over, the new reality is that we can only coach fully vaccinated students. This means, as things currently stand, the lower age for a JOAD student is now 12 years of age. Due to the concerns for safety, there are no exceptions to this rule.
